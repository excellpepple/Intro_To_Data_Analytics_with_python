Room_ID, An exclusive identifier assigned to each room.
Room_Type, It specifies the types of rooms.
Address, (City, State, Country), The address of the rental properties.
Reviews, Total number of reviews for the rental properties.
Satisfaction, (Overall), The level of satisfaction reported by the renters for the Airbnb property, measured on a scale ranging from 1 to 5.
Accommodates, Capacity of the rental properties to accommodate guests.
Bedrooms, Number of bedrooms in the rental properties.
Bathrooms, Number of bathrooms in the rental properties.
Price, The price for a single night's stay.
Latitude, The geographical coordinate representing the north-south position of the Airbnb location.
Longitude, The geographical coordinate indicating the east-west position of the Airbnb location.
